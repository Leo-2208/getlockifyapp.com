A full-featured vault that respects your privacy by design, not as an afterthought.

Encrypted Vault

Files are encrypted before being written to storage — invisible to other apps and system file browsers, even with physical device access.

Folders & Organization

Create folders to keep files organized your way. Easy to navigate, rename, and rearrange — all within the secure vault.

Recycle Bin

Deleted files land in the bin first, auto-purged after 30 days. A simple safety net for accidental deletions.

Biometric Auth

Unlock with fingerprint or face unlock. Android handles all biometrics — Lockify only receives a pass or fail result.

In-App File Viewer

View images, videos, and PDFs directly inside the app. Temporary decrypted copies are cleaned up automatically after viewing.

No Account to Start

No sign-up, no email required to use the vault. Set a PIN, use biometrics, done. Optional account features may be added in future versions.
